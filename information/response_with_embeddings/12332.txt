#### 3. Roles

Key roles are defined to clarify responsibilities and ensure effective project governance and delivery:

*   **Project Sponsor:** Responsible for providing overall project direction, securing resources, and making strategic decisions [Ref 11].
*   **Project Manager:** Responsible for planning, executing, and closing the project within the defined parameters of scope, time, budget, and quality.
*   **Project Board:** Responsible for providing project direction and control, including reviewing progress, approving changes, and ensuring project success.
*   **Senior User(s):** Responsible for specifying user needs and ensuring the project delivers a solution that meets those needs.
*   **Senior Supplier(s):** Responsible for providing the resources and expertise to deliver the project outputs.
*   **Project Assurance:** Provides independent oversight of the project to ensure that it complies with the PRINCE2 methodology and quality standards.
*   **Team Managers:** Responsible for managing the day-to-day work of the teams delivering the project outputs.
