*   **Project Board:** This group typically includes the Sponsor, a Senior User, and a Senior Supplier. The Project Board is responsible for the overall direction and control of the project.

#### 5. Decision Gates

*   **Stage Gate Reviews:** Reviews at the end of each stage to evaluate progress, address issues, and authorize the next stage. Decision gates at the end of each stage, where the project board confirms that the project is still viable and provides continued authorization [Ref 8].
