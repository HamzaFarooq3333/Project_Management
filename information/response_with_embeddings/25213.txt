   i. **Performance Measurement:** Track project progress against the plan (schedule, budget, scope). [Ref 1].
   ii. **Variance Analysis:** Analyze deviations from the plan and take corrective actions.
   iii. **Schedule Control:** Monitor and manage the project schedule.
   iv. **Cost Control:** Monitor and manage the project budget.
   v. **Scope Verification:** Verify completed deliverables and ensure they meet requirements.
   vi. **Quality Control:** Inspect and verify deliverables for quality.
   vii. **Change Control:** Manage and control changes to the project scope, schedule, and budget.
   viii. **Risk Monitoring:** Monitor and manage project risks [Ref 9].
   ix. **Issue Tracking:** Resolve and document project issues.

IV. **Delivery & Closure**

   i. **Deliverable Acceptance:** Obtain formal acceptance of project deliverables from stakeholders [Ref 7].
   ii. **Project Documentation:** Finalize and archive all project documentation.
   iii. **Final Budget Reconciliation:** Ensure all project expenses are accounted for.
   iv. **Lessons Learned:** Conduct a lessons-learned workshop to identify what worked well and what could be improved.
   v. **Project Closure:** Formally close the project, release resources, and archive project documentation [Ref 6].
