#### II. Phases and Activities

**II. Planning Phase**

i.  **Scope Management:** Develop a detailed scope statement, including a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) to define project deliverables [Ref 4].
ii.  **Schedule Management:** Create a detailed project schedule, including activities, dependencies, and resource assignments [Ref 1].
iii. **Cost Management:** Establish a budget and develop a cost management plan [Ref 1].
iv.  **Quality Management:** Define quality standards and develop a quality management plan. [Ref 6]
v.  **Resource Management:** Identify and allocate resources, including human resources, equipment, and materials. [Ref 1]
vi.  **Communications Management:** Refine the communications management plan, detailing communication frequency, methods, and responsibilities [Ref 1].
vii. **Risk Management:** Conduct a detailed risk assessment, develop risk responses, and create a risk register [Ref 8].
viii. **Procurement Planning:** Plan for any required procurement activities [Ref 1].
ix.  **Develop change management plan:** Document the process for handling change requests. [Ref 1]
x.  **Finalize the Marketing Campaign Strategy:**  Refine the campaign strategy, including target audiences, messaging, and channels.

**III. Execution Phase**

i.  **Campaign Launch and Implementation:** Execute the marketing campaign according to the plan.
ii.  **Resource Management:** Manage and monitor project resources.
iii. **Quality Assurance:** Implement the quality management plan.
iv.  **Issue Management:** Identify and resolve project issues as they arise [Ref 13].
v.  **Communications Management:** Execute the communications management plan, providing regular updates to stakeholders.
vi. **Change Requests and Implementation:** Track all change requests through the change management process and implement approved changes.
vii. **Progress Reporting and Monitoring:** Regularly monitor progress against the project plan and key performance indicators (KPIs).

**IV. Monitoring & Controlling Phase**

i.  **Performance Reporting:** Track key performance indicators (KPIs) and project performance against the plan.
ii.  **Variance Analysis:** Analyze variances from the plan and take corrective actions.
iii. **Risk Monitoring and Control:** Monitor risks, implement risk responses, and update the risk register [Ref 8].
iv.  **Change Control:** Manage change requests and implement approved changes [Ref 1].

**V. Closure Phase**

iv.  **Stakeholder Sign-Off:** Obtain stakeholder sign-off on project deliverables.
v.  **Team Debriefing and Lessons Learned:** Conduct a lessons learned session to capture insights and best practices.
