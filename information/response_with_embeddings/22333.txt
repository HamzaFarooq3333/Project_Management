#### V. Decision Gates

The following are key decision gates throughout the project lifecycle:

i.  **Project Charter Approval:** Formal approval of the Project Charter [Ref 11], signifying the project’s official initiation (Project Sponsor).
ii.  **Project Plan Approval:** Approval of the comprehensive Project Management Plan [Ref 1] before execution (Project Sponsor, Project Manager).
iii. **Phase Gate Reviews:** Reviews at the end of each major phase (e.g., design completion, construction phase completion) to assess progress, adherence to requirements, and readiness for the next phase (Project Manager, Construction Manager, Architect, Key Stakeholders).
iv.  **Change Request Approval:** Formal approval of change requests before implementation [Ref 1] (Project Manager, Sponsor, relevant stakeholders).
v.  **Deliverable Acceptance:** Formal acceptance of deliverables by the client upon completion (Client, Project Manager, Stakeholders).
vi.  **Project Closure Approval:** Final approval of project closure after all deliverables are accepted, contracts are closed, and lessons learned are documented (Project Sponsor, Project Manager).
