 Roles

LXI.   Project Manager: Leads and manages the overall project, ensures adherence to the plan, manages stakeholders, risks, communications, and is accountable for project success.
LXII.   Technical Lead: Responsible for the technical architecture, design, configuration, integration, and overall technical quality of the solution. Guides the technical team.
LXIII.   QA Engineer: Defines quality standards, develops test plans, executes tests (unit, integration, system), tracks defects, and ensures the solution meets specified requirements and quality criteria.
LXIV.   Domain Stakeholder (Education): Represents the end-users (faculty, students, administrators). Provides requirements, participates in UAT, and ensures the solution meets educational needs.
LXV.   Sponsor: Provides executive support, approves funding, makes high-level decisions, resolves major roadblocks, and champions the project.
LXVI.   [Optional] Business Analyst: (Could be combined with PM/Domain Stakeholder for small projects) Responsible for detailed requirements gathering, analysis, and documentation.
