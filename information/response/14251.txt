XV.   Activity: Conduct User Training: Domain Stakeholder (A, for ensuring effectiveness), TL (R, for technical content), PM (R, for coordinating).

II.   Assessment: These are crucial gates, well-placed for a waterfall-like methodology implicit here.

IV.   Gate 1: Project Charter Approval (Current - Confirms project initiation and PM authority).

VI.   Gate 3: Design/Architecture Approval: (After Planning, before significant build) Formal approval of the proposed technical architecture and design.
VII.   Gate 4: Configuration/Build Completion Review (Current - After core build, before intensive testing).
VIII.   Gate 5: User Acceptance Testing (UAT) Sign-off: (Crucial for marketing/education projects) Formal acceptance by the business/domain stakeholders that the solution meets their needs.
IX.   Gate 6: Final Validation & Go-Live Approval (Current - Before deployment).
X.   Gate 7: Post-Implementation Review / Lessons Learned Approval: (After project closure) Ensures organizational learning.
