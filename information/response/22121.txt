The core issue is a significant mismatch between the stated project type ("small-scale construction") and the detailed content. XIV. V. Decision Gates

I.   Critique: "Configuration/Build Completion Review" and "Go-Live Approval" are software terms.
II.   Recommendation: Revise to construction-specific milestones.

III.   Gate 1: Project Charter & Feasibility Approval
IV.   Purpose: Formal approval to proceed with detailed planning based on project definition, preliminary scope, and budget.
V.   Approvers: Sponsor, Client Representative, Project Manager.
VI.   Gate 2: Detailed Design & Permitting Approval (Go-Ahead for Construction)
VII.   Purpose: Review and approval of all detailed designs, engineering plans, and confirmation of all necessary permits and regulatory approvals obtained. This is the green light to break ground.
VIII.   Approvers: Sponsor, Client Representative, Architect/Engineer, Project Manager.
IX.   Gate 3: Substantial Completion & Commissioning Review
X.   Purpose: Verify that the project is substantially complete, all major systems are installed and functioning, and it meets the contractual requirements, prior to final punch list work.
XI.   Approvers: Client Representative, Project Manager, Quality Inspector, Architect/Engineer.
XII.   Gate 4: Final Acceptance & Handover Approval
XIII.   Purpose: Formal acceptance of the completed project by the client, including resolution of all punch list items, delivery of as-built documentation, and transfer of warranties.
XIV.   Approvers: Client Representative, Sponsor, Project Manager.
