XIII.   5. Closure: "Deliver documentation and user training." - "User training" is typically for IT systems. For construction, closure involves handover, occupancy permits, warranty, O&M manuals, final payments.

XIV.   Recommendation (Crucial based on Project Type):

XXII.   Monitoring: Include defect management, issue resolution, performance monitoring, scope change management.
XXIII.   Closure: Add post-implementation review, knowledge transfer to support teams, warranty period management.

XXXVII.   Recommendation:

XL.   If Construction: Redefine activities and assign RACI accordingly, involving construction-specific roles. For example, the Construction Manager/Superintendent would be 'A' or 'R' for much of the on-site execution.
